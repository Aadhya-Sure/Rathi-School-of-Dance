About Bharatanatyam

Bharatanatyam is one of the oldest classical dance forms of India, originating in the temples of Tamil Nadu. It is renowned for its grace, purity, tenderness, and sculptural poses.

Our curriculum covers both Nritta (pure rhythmic dance) and Nritya (expressive dance incorporating hand gestures/mudras and facial expressions), guiding students safely from beginner steps to their formal graduation performance (Arangetram).
